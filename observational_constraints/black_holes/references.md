# Black Hole Observational References

## AT2020afhd Disk–Jet Precession

Coughlin, E. R., et al.  
"Detection of disk–jet coprecession in a tidal disruption event."  
Science Advances (2025).  
DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.ady9068

This work reports a 19.6-day quasi-periodic modulation in X-ray and radio emission
from the tidal disruption event AT2020afhd, interpreted as disk–jet co-precession
consistent with Lense–Thirring frame dragging.

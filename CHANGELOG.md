## uetcref-v1.2 (2026-02-01)

### Added
- Galactic-scale observational constraints category populated under `observational_constraints/galaxies/`.
- Archived exploratory: density-gradient inference scaler (global rotation curves).
- Archived exploratory: illustrative NGC 2403 overlay.
- Updated observational-constraints indices to reflect partial population of the Galaxies category.

### Notes
- These additions expand the observational scope of UETC to galactic scales without modifying core propagation definitions.
- All galactic entries are explicitly exploratory, non-unique, and intended to motivate falsifiable future tests.
- No precision fitting, parameter inference, or claims of explanatory completeness are introduced.
